About Me

Hey there, I'm Miguel Cruzeiro, a 22-year-old from Viana do Castelo. I've got a degree in Computing Engineering and Multimedia. My jam is Graphic and Web Design, Post-Production, and Editing.

I'm a tech geek who loves keeping up with all the cool new stuff and having fun discussions about it online. But, you know what I'm really passionate about? Video games, TV shows, and movies – I'm always on the lookout for the latest and greatest.

I studied Computing and Multimedia Engineering at IPVC (Instituto Politécnico de Viana do Castelo).

In my downtime, you'll catch me hanging out with friends, watching sports, gaming, taking photos, and just chilling with my dog.
